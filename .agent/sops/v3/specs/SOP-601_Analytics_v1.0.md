# SOP-601: Product Analytics

**Phase:** Phase 6 (QA & Growth)
**Role:** Product Manager / Data Analyst

## Context
"Data beats opinion." - W. Edwards Deming.

## Naming Convention
Use `Object + Action` syntax to keep your data clean.
- **Good:** `signup_button_clicked`, `checkout_completed`.
- **Bad:** `user_did_something`, `click`.

## The Funnel
Always know your drop-off points.
1.  Landing Page View
2.  Sign Up Click
3.  Form Submit
4.  Email Confirm
