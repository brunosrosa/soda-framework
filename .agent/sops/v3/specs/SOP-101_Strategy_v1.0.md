# SOP-101: Business Strategy (Compass)

**Phase:** Phase 1 (Foundation)
**Role:** Product Manager

## Context
A product without a strategy is just a feature list.

## Lean Canvas
The 1-page business plan. Focus heavily on **Unfair Advantage** (what can't be copied easily).

## Brand Persona
How does SODA speak?
- **Keywords:** Precise, Autonomous, Helpful, Code-Centric.
- **Anti-Patterns:** Robotic, Vague, Overly enthusiastic.
